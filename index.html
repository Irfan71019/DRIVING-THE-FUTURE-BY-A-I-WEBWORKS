Porsche Highlights: Racing, Innovation & Milestones

  • Electrification: The Porsche Taycan (2019) was the first production electric vehicle to use an 800-volt system for ultra-fast charging and high performance.
  • Production Milestones: Over 1 million Porsche 911s have been produced since its launch in 1963, cementing its place as an icon of sports car history.
